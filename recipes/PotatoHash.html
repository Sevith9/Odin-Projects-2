Potato Hash

Description

A delicous and nutrious breakfest option!

Ingredients

  • 2 Golden potatoes
  • 1 cup of baby spinach
  • 2-5 slices of rotessire chicken
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese of your choice
  • 1-3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of garlic poweder
  • 2 tsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of cayanne pepper
  • 1 Tsp of soy sauce
  • 1 Tsp of grapeseed oil

Steps

  1. Dice the two golden potatoes into small cubes
  2. Season with sal,pepper, and onion poweder
  3. Microwave potatoes for 5 min in order to soften them
  4. Cut chicken slices into smaller pieces and season pepper
  5. on medium high heat pan fry pototes until golden brown on all sides
  6. Add chicken to the potatoes and mix to avoid burning
  7. Turn down heat to medium and add baby spinach
  8. Once baby spinach is wilted form 2-3 holes in the hash
  9. Add eggs to the holes formed in the earlier step
  10. Add soy sauce to outer edges of the pan and cover with a lid
  11. *optional flip eggs to fry on both sides
  12. Reduce heat to low and spread shredded cheese over the hash,
