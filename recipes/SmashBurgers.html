Smash Burgers

Description

A juicy smash burger with melted cheese

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 Tsp of butter
  • burger smasher

Steps

  1. Butter and toast buns until golden brown
  2. Roll ground beef into medium sized balls
  3. Generously season with salt and butter
  4. Preheat pan to high heat
  5. Place beef balls onto pan and press down firmly with a flat tool
  6. Season the otherside of the patty
  7. Cook for no more 1-2 min per side or until crust forms
  8. Once you flip the patty add your slice of cheddar cheese
  9. *optional pour a small amount of water(away from the patty) on the skillet and cover with lid to melt cheese
