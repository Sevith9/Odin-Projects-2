Steak & Shrimp

Description

A delicous and filling meal,dripping with intense flavor and intoxicating aromas

Ingredients

  • 1-2 8oz sirolin steak
  • 5-7 large shrimp(deveined, with no tails)
  • 2 Tsp of grapeseed oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic(peeled)
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp of old bay seasoning
  • 4 Tsp of butter
  • Two sprigs of thyme
  • Two sprigs of rosemary

Steps

  1. Pat steak dry with a paper towel and season generously with salt and pepper
  2. Season shrimp with old bay seasoning and let sit 5-10 min
  3. preheat a cast iron skilled on medium-high heat
  4. add grapeseed oil
  5. when oil starts to shimmer, proceed to sear steak for 2-3 min per side
  6. After flipping the steak add garlic and herbs
  7. Then turn the heat off, move the steak to the edge of the pan and add butter to pan
  8. As butter begins to pool, use a spoon to baste the steak. Repeat this on the other side
  9. Set steak aside and let it rest for 5-7 min
  10. Bring the heat back up to medium low and sear the shrimp for a 30-45 secs per side
  11. *optional baste the shrimp in the leftover garlic butter
